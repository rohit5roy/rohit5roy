Koove

Enterprise Web / Mobile App

Koove is first of its kind, app to buy sell used goods, or second hand products in a fun and trusted social platform. We promote trust by ensuring safety.e.g. Real User Profiles, Moderated Community, Chat, Social, Comments, etc. Koove is different from usual used goods classifieds platforms like OLX, Quikr, etc. It is a place to declutter, reduce wastage & save money in a virtual flea market experience.
